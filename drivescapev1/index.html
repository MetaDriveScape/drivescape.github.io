Recent advancements in generative models offer promising solutions to synthesize realistic driving videos, aiding in training autonomous driving perception model. However, existing approaches often fall short in multi-view video generation due to challenges in integrating 3D information while maintaining spatial-temporal consistency, and learning by a unified model effectively. In this paper, We propose an end-to-end framework named DriveScape for multi-view 3D condition-guided video generation. DriveScape not only streamlines the process by integrating camera information for comprehensive spatial-temporal coverage but also introduces a Bi-Directional modulated transformer module to align various 3D road structural information effectively. Consequently, our approach facilitates precise control over video generation, greatly enhancing realism and offering a robust solution for creating multi-view driving videos. Our framework has achieved state-of-the-art results on the nuScenes dataset, demonstrating impressive generative quality metrics with an FID score of 8.34 and an FVD score of 87.78, as well as superior performance across various perception tasks. This lays the foundation for more accurate environment simulation in autonomous driving. We will make our code and pre-trained model publicly.
